How CTU sell-out is calculated
DS enters current stock count on every visit. System compares with previous visit count for the same outlet.
Units sold = Previous stock count − Current stock count
Example: Visit 1 → DS enters 15 units. Visit 2 → DS enters 9 units. Sell-out = 6 units sold between the two visits.
